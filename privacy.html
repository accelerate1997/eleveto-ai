At Elevetoai, we collect information necessary to provide specialized AI development services and maintain business portals. This includes:

2. AI Data Processing

As an AI-first agency, we handle data with specialized care:

  • Data used to train or fine-tune specialized AI agents is isolated and encrypted.
  • We prioritize on-device or private-cloud processing to minimize data exposure.

3. Security Measures

We implement enterprise-grade security architecture:

  • End-to-end encryption for all proprietary business data.
  • Regular security audits of our custom business portals.
  • Strict access controls for our development and operations teams.

4. Data Ownership & Rights

You maintain full ownership of your intellectual property and project data. You have the right to request:

  • Access to your data stored in our systems.
  • Correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion of data once a project is completed and handed over.

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact our security team at privacy@elevetoai.com.
